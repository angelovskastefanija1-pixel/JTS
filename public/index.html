What We Are



JTS Logistics Inc is the leading and fast growing logistics company in the Midwest.
JTS Logistics operates as a 100% Owner Operator company, meaning it does not own any trucks, but rather possesses equipment such as Dry Vans, Flat Beds, and reefers. The company was founded by three friends who have accumulated over 20 years of experience in the industry as truck drivers, dispatchers, and more, thus gaining extensive knowledge.


We are a trucking company DEDICATED TO PROFESSIONALISM
Dependable Service, Customized Solutions, Cutting-Edge Tracking Technology, striving to deliver the highest quality service to our clients.



What we provide for our Owner Operators
We allocate a dedicated dispatcher to each owner operator, treating them like family. We consistently seek the most suitable and highest-paying loads for them, maintaining communication at the highest level to ensure they receive a good paycheck by the end of the week.


No contracts. Cancel anytime. You keep full control of your schedule.
